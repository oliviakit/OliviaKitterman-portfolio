A life of dreaming About Olivia Kitterman

Olivia was born and raised in Utah. She started writing at an early age and finished her first novel in middle school. After meeting editors at a local writing conference, she discovered a love for editing. She is currently attending Brigham Young University to become a professional editor.

In her freetime, Olivia loves to read in trees, participate in choirs, and go hiking in the mountains. She is currently obsessed with The Wheel of Time and The Stormlight Archives.
